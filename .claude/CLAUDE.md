## Project Overview

This is a startup competition project for **Mathify** (mathify.io), an AI-powered K-12 math education SaaS platform. The competition is **"創業綻放-創業大聯盟競賽"** organized by the National Development Council (國家發展委員會).

- **Founder**: 劉明燁 (Bennie Liu), bennie.liu@mathify.io
- **Category**: 生活驅動-消費生活組 | 非六都
- **Current Stage**: Passed preliminary round (初賽), preparing for semi-finals (複賽)

## Competition Timeline & Deliverables

- **Semi-final deadline**: 115年3月31日 (March 31, 2026) — submit **優化商業簡報** + **商業計畫書**
- **Semi-final evaluation**: 115年4月1日 ~ 5月15日 — in-person review, select up to 100 winning teams
- **Finals**: Top 30 teams compete for 總統獎 (NT$1,000萬) and 院長獎 (NT$600萬); others receive 綻放獎 (NT$300萬)

### Five Scoring Criteria (評分項目)

1. **解決方案** — market pain points, product/service, innovation, core competitiveness, competitor comparison
2. **市場分析** — industry overview, market size, expansion potential, internationalization
3. **商業模式** — business model design and feasibility
4. **團隊潛力** — founder experience, team collaboration, execution capability
5. **財務規劃與管理** — fund allocation plan based on NT$300萬, with at least 3 quantifiable KPIs

## Mathify Product & Business Context

- **Product**: AI-driven one-stop math learning platform for junior/senior high school students
- **Tech differentiator**: Fine-tuned LLM with TikZ/Desmos integration for visual math problem-solving
- **Target market**: 1,080,000 junior/senior high students in Taiwan; 648,000 potential paying users (60% tutoring rate)
- **Business model**: Freemium SaaS — free tier with usage limits, paid tier at ~NT$600/month
- **Revenue target**: 38,880 paying users (6% market share) = NT$2.8億/year
- **Team**: 劉明燁 (Founder, 20+ yrs math education, NTHU Math + NTU Stats) and 石亞喬 (CPTO, AI/algorithm + math education)

### First-Year KPIs (from preliminary pitch)

- 500 paying users (6% conversion)
- 30% weekly active user ratio
- 70% AI problem-solving feature usage
- NT$180萬 annual revenue
